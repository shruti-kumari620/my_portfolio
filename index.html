About Me

Hello! I am an Assistant Professor and AI Researcher with a strong background in Data Science and Computer Science. I am currently pursuing my M.Tech in Data Science from Amity University, Haryana (CGPA: 9.33), building upon my B.Tech in CSE (CGPA: 9.52).

With over 2+ years of combined industry and academic experience, I have worked as a Project Engineer at Wipro Technologies, and completed impactful internships at HackTech and the Wargaming Development Center (Indian Army). My passion lies in mentoring students, simplifying complex technical concepts, and translating raw data into actionable strategic insights.

My current research focuses on the intersection of Classical and Quantum Machine Learning, particularly in healthcare analytics, with accepted publications in reputed Springer conferences and books.
